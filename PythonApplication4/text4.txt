The band met when they were students at University College London (UCL) in September 1996 . Martin and Buckland were the original Coldplay members who met during university dating week. They spent the rest of the academic year planning the formation of a band called Pectoralz. Later, one of their classmates, Guy Berryman , became a member of the band. In 1997 the band was renamed Starfish. In the same year they gave concerts in various small clubs in Camden to attract the attention of various managers. Martin approached his old classmate Phil Harvey, who was taking a classical studies at Oxford University., and asked him to become the band manager. Since that day, Phil is considered the fifth member of Coldplay.
